\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
implements three FIR low-\/pass filters 

Finite length LPF~\newline
 1) Size is the depth 2 to 512~\newline
 2) y(n) = (sum(x(n)+x(n-\/1)+...+x(n-\/size-\/1))/size~\newline
 3) To use a filter~\newline
 a) initialize it once~\newline
 b) call the filter at the sampling rate~\newline
